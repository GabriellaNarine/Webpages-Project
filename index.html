Why Strong Passwords Matter

Strong passwords are your first line of defense against hackers and cybercriminals. Weak or commonly used passwords can be easily cracked, leading to identity theft, financial loss, and exposure of your private information.

According to SeacoastBank, there are over 2,200 cyberattacks per day! This emphasizes how important it is to create strong, secure passwords to protect your online accounts.

By understanding the risks, you can take simple steps to safeguard your personal and financial data online.
